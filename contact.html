FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders are processed within 24 hours and delivered within 2-5 business days, depending on your location. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging. Some special order or custom items may not be eligible for return. Please contact our support team for assistance with returns.

Are your parts compatible with my vehicle?

Our website features a vehicle compatibility checker on most product pages. Simply enter your vehicle's make, model, and year to see if the part is compatible. If you're still unsure, our technical support team is available to help verify compatibility.

Do you offer installation services?

We don't provide direct installation services, but we have a network of trusted partner shops in major cities. We can provide installation guides and technical support for DIY installations, and we're happy to recommend local professionals for complex jobs.

Are the parts covered by warranty?

Yes, most of our parts come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product and brand, typically ranging from 1 to 5 years. Warranty information is listed on each product page, and our customer service team can provide additional details.
